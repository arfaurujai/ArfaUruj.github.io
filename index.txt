# about

About me

Hi, I’m Mohammed Arfa Uruj, a Full Stack Developer and AI Engineer with hands-on experience building scalable web and mobile applications across finance, healthcare, retail, and logistics. I recently completed my Master’s in Computer Science at California State University, San Bernardino, where I focused on AI/ML, blockchain, and advanced system design.

Over the past six years, I’ve worked on projects ranging from real-time financial dashboards at FactSet, HIPAA-compliant healthcare systems at Premera Blue Cross, to retail inventory platforms at O’Reilly Auto Parts. Alongside industry roles, I’ve also built passion projects like a Smart Fridge app with barcode scanning and AI recipes, an AI-powered task breakdown app, and a Chrome extension for automated attendance tracking using facial recognition.

I specialize in React, Angular, Node.js, Spring Boot, and cloud deployment (AWS, Azure, Supabase), with a growing focus on AI agents, RAG systems, and LLM-powered applications. I enjoy tackling problems that blend performance, security, and user experience while exploring creative ways to make technology more impactful.

When I’m not coding, I’m often experimenting with AI-driven side projects, writing about emerging tech, or gaming (yes, I’ll happily talk Warframe builds or Pokémon strategies). My goal is to continue building solutions that combine innovation, usability, and AI-driven intelligence—whether that’s for enterprise systems or consumer-focused apps.
